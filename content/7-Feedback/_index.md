### Overall Program Evaluation

**1. Impressions of the Program and Learning Environment**
* The First Cloud AI Journey program creates an open and collaborative tech community rather than a rigid corporate routine. It is the perfect bridge for students to transition applications from local development to production-ready deployments on AWS Cloud.
* The AWS Vietnam office workspace is professional, modern, and comfortable, encouraging interns to focus on project execution and technical design.

**2. Support from Mentors and Admin Team**
* Mentors were highly supportive, offering technical guidance and architectural advice even outside official hours. I deeply appreciate the self-driven training approach, where mentors allow interns to experiment with designs before suggesting options, enhancing problem-solving capabilities.
* The administrative team was responsive, assisting with workspace access and providing AWS sandbox accounts for hands-on labs.

**3. Alignment with Academic Major**
* The serverless game project directly matches my Software Engineering curriculum. It reinforced my core programming foundation while introducing cloud-native practices (NoSQL with DynamoDB, real-time channels with API Gateway WebSocket, and CloudFront edge security) that are not deeply covered in academic coursework.

**4. Overall Program Satisfaction**
* I am highly satisfied with the structured internship roadmap — starting with fundamental hands-on labs and culminating in the free-form design and deployment of our Battleship Arena game project.

---

### Responses to Additional Questions

* **What did you find most satisfying during the internship?**
  * Having hands-on access to build a full serverless web application on AWS Cloud, attending technical meetups (FCAJ Community Days), and networking with local cloud experts.
* **What areas should the program improve for future cohorts?**
  * The physical office booking system could be better balanced. Currently, random selection leaves some interns with fewer office visits. Standardizing allocation would ensure equal opportunities for all participants to collaborate in person.
* **Would you recommend this program to a friend?**
  * Definitely yes. It is one of the most practical internships available for students to gain real-world cloud experience, providing access to quality resources and high-level tech mentorship.

### Suggestions & Future Expectations

* **Experience Improvement:** I suggest organizing joint social meetups or team-bonding activities across different project groups to strengthen community relationships.
* **Future Intentions:** I hope to remain connected with the FCAJ community and would love to contribute in the future as a mentor assisting new cohorts or participate in subsequent cloud projects.
